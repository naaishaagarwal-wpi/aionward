# AI Onward

**A youth-led movement helping people of every generation understand, explore, and apply AI.**

🌐 Website: [https://aionward.org](https://aionward.org)

---

## About AI Onward

AI Onward was created from a simple belief:

> **Everyone belongs in the future of AI.**

What started as three separate efforts — **AI Visionaries**, **Girls Applying AI**, and **AI For Seniors** — grew into one movement focused on making AI more approachable, inclusive, and human.

AI Onward brings together students, educators, families, and communities to explore AI through learning, conversations, workshops, and shared experiences.

We believe AI should not belong only to experts.

**AI belongs to everyone.**

---



# Our Programs



## AI Visionaries

AI Visionaries began as a student-led initiative exploring how artificial intelligence can be understood and applied across different fields.

Through discussions, projects, competitions, and conversations with experts, students explore AI beyond coding — including its applications, opportunities, and impact on society.

Focus areas include:

- AI literacy
- Real-world AI applications
- Responsible AI conversations
- Student-led exploration and projects

---



## Girls Applying AI

Girls Applying AI was created from observing a gap in confidence, access, and representation for girls exploring artificial intelligence and technology.

The initiative aims to encourage more girls to discover AI, build confidence, and see themselves as creators of future technologies.

Focus areas include:

- AI exploration
- Community building
- Encouraging young women in technology
- Creating opportunities to learn and collaborate

---



## AI For Seniors

AI For Seniors began after seeing family members and older generations feel uncertain about how to approach artificial intelligence.

Through hands-on workshops and conversations, AI Onward helps seniors understand practical AI applications and feel more comfortable using emerging technologies.

Focus areas include:

- Introduction to AI concepts
- Practical everyday AI applications
- Hands-on learning
- Intergenerational conversations

# Website Technology

This repository contains the AI Onward website.

Built with:

- Next.js
- TypeScript
- Tailwind CSS
- shadcn/ui

The website uses a content-driven architecture designed for future growth and CMS integration.

---



# Architecture

The website separates content, data access, and presentation layers.

Current structure:
Content
  ↓
Content Access Layer
  ↓
Reusable Components
  ↓
Website Pages

This approach allows AI Onward to evolve from local content files to a future content management system without rebuilding the website.

---



## Development Principles

The AI Onward website follows these principles:

### Human first

Technology should help people, not intimidate them.

### Stories over hype

The focus is on real people, real learning, and real experiences.

### Inclusive by design

AI education should be approachable for people of every generation and background.

### Built to grow

The website architecture is designed to support future programs, workshops, resources, and community contributions.
