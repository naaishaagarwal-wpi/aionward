# AI Onward Project Context

## Overview

AI Onward is a youth-led community helping people explore, understand, and participate in the future of AI.

AI Onward believes:

> Everyone belongs in the future of AI.

The goal is not to make AI feel exclusive, technical, or intimidating. The goal is to make AI accessible, creative, and human.

# Voice and Writing Style

## Use

- AI Onward is a community...

- People belong here.

- Curiosity comes first.

- Everyone can participate.

- AI is something we explore together.

## Avoid

Do not write:

- "We are building..."

- "We provide solutions..."

- "Our organization..."

- corporate nonprofit language

- generic startup language

AI Onward should not sound like a company, consultancy, or traditional nonprofit.

# Design Direction

## Overall Feeling

The website should feel like:

- a magazine cover

- an Instagram story

- a community journal

- a collection of human moments

Not:

- SaaS landing page

- corporate website

- nonprofit brochure

---

# Visual Principles

## Colors

Primary:

- white background

- black typography

Accent:

- occasional handwritten highlights

- small colorful strokes

- human marks

Avoid:

- large color blocks

- gradients

- excessive decoration

---

# Typography

Use:

- clean modern typography for main content

- handwritten style only for accents

- personal notes

- observations

- human comments

Not:

- school notebook decoration
